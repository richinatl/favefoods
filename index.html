Five great Atlanta East

The List

Homegrown

What I like

  • The southern comfort biscuit is pure heaven

  • Exceptional value

  • Laid back atmosphere

Community Q

What I like

  • Pull pork that is the most tender you'll put in your mouth

  • The mac and cheese, unhealty as hell, & worth every bit of it

  • Pro tip - arrive early, it takes hours to smoke bbq & sometimes they run out

Bangkok Thai

What I like

  • Thai food that's actually spicy like it should be, not for the faint of heart but perfect for a Thai lover

  • Great ambiance, just a great place for a cozy meal

  • Always clean & service is on point

The Vortex

What I like

  • While you might get a better burger in the city, none are served in such a fucnky & fun atmosphere

  • A huge vareity of options for any burger craving

  • My preferred location is little 5 points, I always have fun hanging out around there

El Rey Del Taco

What I like

  • My absolute favorite tacos in the city

  • You can get pitchers of margarits for those fun times out with friends

  • Spend the extra for the big tacos, the homemade tortillas are worth it!

Who am I

No one special really. Just a guy who loves food & loves our great local restaurants. Growing up where I did, there weren't many options. When I moved to Atlanta 20 years ago I was amazed at all the things I'd never tried. And as I've traveled the world I have appreciated even more how many unique spots we have in Atlanta run by some truly wonderful people. There's a lot wrong in this world now, but one thing we can agree on is the way a good meal is good for the soul, and being out with friends sharing a meal & laughing together is one of the best ways to refresh your whole being
